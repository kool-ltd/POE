Lean Canvas: Five Elements Customizable Water Bottle

1. Problems

  • Generic bottles lack personalization and cultural meaning
  • Single-material bottles can't adapt to mood/luck cycles
  • Gift buyers struggle to find eco-friendly, meaningful presents

Existing Alternatives: Standard plastic/steel bottles, one-off engraved bottles, single-material thermoses

4. Solutions

  • Universal transparent Tritan™ shell + five element liners
  • Quick-swap inner-liner mechanism
  • Custom engraving/UV printing

3. Unique Value Proposition

One bottle, five elements: interchangeable eco-material liners + custom engraving.
