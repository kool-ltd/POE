Lean Canvas: Kitchen Canvas Print-On-Demand Surface

1. Problems

  • Generic cutting boards feel impersonal and stale
  • No easy way to refresh kitchen décor with seasonal or custom art
  • Durable DIY stickers/decals aren’t dishwasher-safe and peel or fade
  • Gift market demands unique, functional art pieces

Existing Alternatives: plain cutting boards; vinyl decals and stickers; decorative mats; single-use printed placemats

4. Solutions

  • On-demand UV printing fused into the prep board—art, measurement guides, recipes, flavor maps
  • Web/Mobile Design Studio with live 3D preview, template library, and custom uploads
  • Curated artist and brand marketplace with seasonal drops and limited-edition collaborations
  • Functional overlays: measurement charts, mini-recipes, and flavor wheels

3. Unique Value Proposition

“Your Kitchen Canvas: Where Function Meets Your Personality”

Personalize a dishwasher-safe prep board with art or functional prints, swap designs seasonally, and join a creative cooking community.

9. Unfair Advantage

  • Proprietary, FDA-safe UV-print process embedded into board—no peeling or fading
  • First-mover in print-on-demand cutting boards with both art and functional overlays
  • In-house 3D live-preview Design Studio for seamless customization
  • Exclusive network of artists and limited-edition collaborations
  • Zero-inventory, sustainable on-demand production

2. Customer Segments

  • Aesthetic-minded home cooks (ages 25–45) seeking personalization in the kitchen
  • Gift shoppers (weddings, housewarmings, holidays) desiring unique, usable art
  • Brands & organizations: cooking schools, meal-kit services, restaurants, corporate gifting programs
  • Artists and designers looking for a kitchen-based monetization channel

8. Key Metrics

  • Subscription take-rate and churn
  • Design uploads per customer
  • Percentage of orders featuring marketplace or seasonal designs
  • Customer social shares of custom boards (photos & videos)
  • Repeat purchase rate and average revenue per user
  • Number and revenue share from B2B partnerships

5. Channels

  • Own website with integrated Design Studio for direct customization and sales
  • Amazon Marketplace to reach broader kitchenware shoppers
  • Social media ads on Instagram & Pinterest targeting kitchen décor and custom gifts
  • Influencer partnerships showcasing design and cooking demos
  • Co-branded offerings through cooking schools, meal-kit companies, and corporate events

7. Cost Structure

  • Board manufacturing, integrated prep-tool COGS, and UV-print consumables
  • Fulfillment, warehousing, and shipping expenses
  • Platform development and hosting for Design Studio and marketplace
  • Marketing, influencer collaborations, and community management
  • B2B account management and custom design service costs

6. Revenue Streams

  • Sale of core prep board (includes up to 3 free designs)
  • Quarterly seasonal design subscription
  • Premium custom-upload fees ($7–12 per design)
  • Marketplace commission (25%) on third-party and artist designs
  • B2B licensing and co-branding fees
  • Limited-edition and collaborative design premium sales
