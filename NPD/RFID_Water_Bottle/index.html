Lean Canvas: RFID Water Bottle Refill Network

1. Problems

  • Excessive single-use plastic bottle waste
  • No consistent incentives for reusable bottle refills
  • Uneven access to free/discounted drinking water

Existing Alternatives: Buying bottled water, public fountains (no rewards), ad-hoc coffee shop refills

4. Solutions

  • Reusable bottles embedded with passive RFID/NFC
  • Mobile app to locate and “tap-to-refill” at partners
  • Partner portal & API for real-time offer management

3. Unique Value Proposition

“Tap. Refill. Save.”

Your smart bottle unlocks free or discounted water & drinks at a network of partner venues—good for you, great for the planet.

9. Unfair Advantage

  • Rapid network effect of participating venues
  • Proprietary tap-event API & POS integrations
  • Exclusive aggregated hydration-habits data
  • First-mover advantage in RFID refill ecosystem

2. Customer Segments

  • Eco-conscious commuters and students
  • Gym-goers, co-workers & office employees
  • Event attendees (concerts, conferences)
  • Cafés, gyms & workplaces seeking green branding

8. Key Metrics

  • Number of active RFID bottles in market
  • Monthly active “tappers” (refill events)
  • Average refills per bottle per month
  • Partner venue onboarding & retention rate
  • Subscription conversion & churn rates

5. Channels

  • Direct B2B sales to cafés, gyms & offices
  • E-commerce & retail distribution of bottles
  • Referral & influencer marketing to users
  • CSR partnerships with municipalities & NGOs

7. Cost Structure

  • Bottle manufacturing & RFID hardware
  • Cloud backend & mobile app development
  • Marketing, sales & partner integration
  • Customer support & operations

6. Revenue Streams

  • One-time bottle sales (retail & wholesale)
  • Premium subscription for unlimited refills
  • Transaction fees on paid refills & upsells
  • Venue analytics & data-as-a-service subscriptions
